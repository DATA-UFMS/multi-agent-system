## Using Social Media Management with Profiles, Groups, LinkedIn Pages or Showcase Pages

Last updated: 4 years ago

The leading social media management companies have developed integrations that can be synced with your profile, Group features, LinkedIn Pages, and Showcase Pages. Please contact your social media management provider for instructions on how you can connect to their product.

**Note:** Social media management refers to products that let you combine the management of all your social media profiles into a single interface, so you can write and schedule posts, manage comments, and monitor analytics from one place.
