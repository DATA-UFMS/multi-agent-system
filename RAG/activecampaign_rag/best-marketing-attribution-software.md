##  What is marketing attribution software? 

Marketing attribution software is designed to analyze the [ marketing campaigns ](https://www.activecampaign.com/blog/marketing-campaign) of a business and determine which touchpoints (such as ads, emails, or social media posts) are most effective in driving conversions. It works by tracking customer behavior across multiple channels and assigning a value to each touchpoint based on its contribution to a sale or conversion. 

This information can be used to optimize future campaigns and allocate marketing resources more effectively. Marketing attribution software typically uses various models to attribute credit to different touchpoints based on their influence. 

For example, a first-click attribution model gives all credit to the first touchpoint a customer encounters. 

In contrast, a last-click attribution model assigns all credit to the final touchpoint, directly leading to a conversion. Other models include linear attribution, which gives equal credit to all touchpoints, and time decay attribution, which assigns more credit to touchpoints closer to the time of conversion. 

Overall, marketing attribution software aims to provide businesses with a better understanding of the customer journey and which marketing efforts are most effective in generating revenue. This allows for more informed and targeted marketing strategies to improve ROI and drive growth. 

##  What are the benefits of marketing attribution software? 

Marketing attribution software has several benefits for businesses that rely on marketing campaigns to drive sales. One of the main benefits is that it can provide a more accurate picture of which marketing tactics are driving revenue so businesses can better allocate their marketing budgets. This can improve ROI, as companies can identify and invest in the most effective [ marketing channels ](https://www.activecampaign.com/blog/channel-marketing) . 

Another benefit of marketing attribution software is that it can improve collaboration between teams. By providing a clear view of the [ marketing funnel ](https://www.activecampaign.com/blog/sales-funnel) and customer interactions with different marketing channels, attribution software can help teams work together to optimize their efforts. 

This can lead to more effective campaigns, as teams can identify the most impactful content and messaging. In addition, marketing attribution software can help businesses better understand their customers. By analyzing data on customer behavior and the marketing channels that lead to sales, companies can gain insights into what motivates their customers and how to better engage with them. 

This can improve customer retention and loyalty, as businesses can better tailor their marketing efforts to their target audience. Overall, marketing attribution software is a powerful tool for businesses looking to improve the effectiveness and ROI of their marketing campaigns. 

##  How to choose the best marketing attribution software? 

Choosing the right marketing attribution software is crucial to identifying the effectiveness and ROI of your marketing campaigns. The primary considerations for selecting the right marketing attribution software for your business include the complexity of branding and sales channels, the software's data capture capabilities, the software's comprehensiveness, and the software's cost. 

First, businesses with multiple branding and sales channels require a marketing attribution tool to track and analyze data from various channels, such as social media, email, and online ads. This will help achieve better insights, which will drive a more effective marketing plan. 

Second, the data capture capabilities of the marketing attribution software are vital, as it needs to accurately capture the source of inbound leads and track referrals to give the right ROI figures. 

Third, the comprehensiveness of the software should cover all key marketing areas, including social media marketing, [ email marketing ](https://www.activecampaign.com/feature/email-marketing) , and other online marketing methods. 

Finally, the cost of the software is an important consideration, as you’ll want to ensure you get your money's worth from the tool. The pricing of marketing attribution software ranges greatly depending on the features you need, so ensure that you invest in a product that is affordable and with the features required for your business.
