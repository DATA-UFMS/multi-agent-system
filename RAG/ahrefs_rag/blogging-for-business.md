#  Blogging for business 

Blogging for Business is a free blogging course where you'll learn how to start and grow a blog past 100,000 visitors and turn those readers into customers. 

Watch intro 

![SEO Training](https://frontend-eu.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/blogging-for-business-course-big-BDBLHPPL_84a822f21733039401f57ddfea398ee7_d939686ef5395415a3043c2db016b2ae.jpg)

Who is this course for? 

  * Content marketing professionals 

  * Bloggers and freelance writers 

  * Digital marketing team leaders 




If you want to drive real business growth through your content marketing efforts, then this course will change the way you do digital marketing. 

40 lessons 

In 11 modules 

4h  \u00A0\u00A0\u00A0  56m 

Total length 

[ Start course  ](/academy/blogging-for-business/lesson-1-1)

No sign-up required, and absolutely free of charge 

##  What you’ll learn 

  * How to create a blogging strategy that attracts potential customers. 

  * The content marketing playbook that helped Ahrefs to grow our organic traffic. 

  * How to get consistent traffic from search engines like Google. 

  * How to find topics for your blog. 

  * How to optimize your articles. 

  * Blog promotion techniques.
