#  How to use Ahrefs 

Learn practical ways to use Ahrefs' SEO tools and reports to improve SEO. 

Watch intro 

![SEO Training](https://frontend-eu.s3.eu-central-1.amazonaws.com/how-to-use-ahrefs-course-big-G5DJBK2N_b21e33f2c4a57362cc5d10a4e43588db_db2fecbf1303c28be55b830e0407cb98.jpg)

###  Who is this course for? 

  * Ahrefs users 

  * SEOs 

  * Digital marketers 




This course is geared towards new and existing users of Ahrefs. However, anyone who is interested in SEO will still be able to learn different tactics to improve their Google rankings and get more search traffic. 

62 lessons 

In 12 modules

##  Get Ahrefs certified 

Turn your Ahrefs knowledge into a tangible asset that opens doors to better roles, bigger clients, and lasting marketing success. 

[ Take Exam  ](/academy/how-to-use-ahrefs/exam) 67 questions

##  What you’ll learn 

  * How to analyze yours and your competitors’ websites with Site Explorer 

  * How to master keyword research with Keywords Explorer 

  * How to improve your on-page and technical SEO with Site Audit 

  * How to track and improve your Google rankings with Rank Tracker 

  * How to discover untapped keyword and link building opportunities with Content Explorer 

  * How to get keyword and link building opportunities on autopilot with Alerts
