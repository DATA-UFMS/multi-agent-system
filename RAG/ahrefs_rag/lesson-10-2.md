About this course  ## [ Blogging for business  ](/academy/blogging-for-business)

40 lessons 

4h 56m 

Blogging for Business is a free blogging course where you'll learn how to start and grow a blog past 100,000 visitors and turn those readers into customers. 

###  What you’ll learn 

  * How to create a blogging strategy that attracts potential customers. 

  * The content marketing playbook that helped Ahrefs to grow our organic traffic. 

  * How to get consistent traffic from search engines like Google. 

  * How to find topics for your blog. 

  * How to optimize your articles. 

  * Blog promotion techniques.
