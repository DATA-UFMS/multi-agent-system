About this course  ## [ SEO Course for Beginners  ](/academy/seo-training-course)

14 lessons 

2h 00m 

It doesn't matter if you know nothing about SEO. By the end of this free SEO training course, you’ll understand how to apply the basics of SEO like keyword research, on-page SEO, and link building to rank higher in search engines. 

###  What you’ll learn 

  * The fundamentals of what search engine optimization is and how it works 

  * Why SEO is important 

  * How to do keyword research 

  * How to optimize web pages for search engines 

  * Beginner-friendly link building strategies to get backlinks to your site 

  * Technical SEO best practices for beginners
