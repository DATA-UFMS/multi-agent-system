Content Marketing Brand Story (and Brand Stories) 

Learn how to define your brand story and the stories your brand will tell to reinforce it across content channels and platforms. 

> Developing the content in our content marketing strategy is developing the story of the big ideas your brand represents. It’s the differentiated experiences you want to create. It’s what you REALLY do for a living. For better or worse — it’s that simple.

Business storytelling isn't intended to be a “selling” tool; it’s a method of building strong relationships with your customers and a thriving community of loyalists over time. Your story identifies your brand's passions are and serves as the foundation for all your future content developments. 

One way to uncover your organization's “story” is through a  [ point-of-view architecture ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/content-marketing-strategy/facts-won-t-cut-it-how-to-use-point-of-view-architecture-to-create-trust) , which can help you develop a structure that can be used across one small content marketing initiative — or across an entire strategy of content marketing throughout your company.
