##  Remember your influencers 

You may think  [ buyer persona ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/audience-building/the-3-biggest-mistakes-marketers-make-with-buyer-personas) = customer, but there are other influencers you need to consider as well. This short video featuring  [ Amanda Maksymiw ](http://twitter.com/amandamaks) ,  [ Leslie Reiser ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/latest-news) , and  [ Waynette Tubbs ](http://twitter.com/WaynetteTubbs) reminds us that you need to have a plan for reaching industry thought leaders, those within your organization and customers. 

Here are some other informative posts on influencer marketing: 

  * [ How to Turn Influencers Into a Powerful Content Force ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/latest-news)

  * [ 6 Influencer Marketing Metrics To Watch and 5 Tools To Help ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/analytics-data/6-influencer-marketing-metrics-to-watch-and-5-tools-to-help)

  * [ 5 Brands See Big Value in Multi-Billion-Dollar Influencer Marketing Industry ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/social-media-content/5-brands-see-big-value-in-multi-billion-dollar-influencer-marketing-industry)

  * [ 6 Old-School Techniques To Integrate Into Your Influencer Marketing ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/latest-news)

  * [ Influencer Marketing Experts Pick Their Favorite Tools To Manage Their Programs ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/latest-news)

  * [ What Influencers Wish Brands Knew About Sponsored Content Partnerships ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/social-media-content/what-influencers-wish-brands-knew-about-sponsored-content-partnerships)




Want to bring all of this together?  [ A content segmentation grid ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/latest-news) helps you build a high-level view of your different audiences, what content you have that meets their needs, and where your gaps are after your personas have been developed. Use this as a way to understand not only what you’ve developed so far, but where you’ll need to spend the most time as you begin to put your content marketing plans into action.
