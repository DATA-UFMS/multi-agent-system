###  General Email Marketing Statistics and Best Practices 

  * Email marketing is used by 31% of marketers. ( [ HubSpot Blog Research ](/marketing/hubspot-blog-marketing-industry-trends-report) , 2023) 
  * Email marketing revenue is estimated to reach almost 11 billion by the end of 2023. ( [ Statista ](https://www.statista.com/statistics/812060/email-marketing-revenue-worldwide/) , 2021) 



![email marketing stats: graph displaying email marketing revenue through 2027](https://53.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hub/53/hubfs/email%20marketing%20revenue.png?width=1000&name=email%20marketing%20revenue.png)

[ Image Source ](https://www.statista.com/statistics/812060/email-marketing-revenue-worldwide/)

  * There are 4 billion daily email users. This number is expected to climb to 4.6 billion by 2025. ( [ Statista ](https://www.statista.com/statistics/255080/number-of-e-mail-users-worldwide/) , 2021) 
  * More than 347 billion emails are sent and received each day. ( [ Statista ](https://www.statista.com/statistics/456500/daily-number-of-e-mails-worldwide/) , 2022) 
  * 64% of small businesses use email marketing to reach customers. ( [ Campaign Monitor ](https://www.campaignmonitor.com/resources/guides/the-state-of-small-business-marketing/#one) , 2021) 
  * 38% of brands are increasing their email budget, and just 10% are making cuts. ( [ HubSpot Blog Research ](/marketing/hubspot-blog-marketing-industry-trends-report) , 2023) 
  * The most effective strategies for email marketing campaigns are subscriber segmentation (78%), message personalization (72%), and email automation campaigns (71%). ( [ HubSpot Blog Research ](/marketing/hubspot-blog-marketing-industry-trends-report) , 2021) 
  * Emails with multimedia elements or formats with images and/or videos have the highest performance. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2023) 
  * The best time to send a marketing email is 9:00 AM - 12 PM EST, then 12:01 PM - 3 PM EST. The worst is 1 AM - 3 AM EST. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2023) 



![graph displaying the best time to send a marketing email](https://53.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hub/53/hubfs/best%20time%20to%20send.jpg?width=1500&height=844&name=best%20time%20to%20send.jpg)

  * 22% of marketers (or the companies they work for) send marketing emails 2-3 per day, and 21% send daily emails. 12% send a weekly email. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2022) 



![pie chart displaying how often marketers send marketing emails](https://53.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hub/53/hubfs/how%20often%20send.jpg?width=700&height=394&name=how%20often%20send.jpg)

  


  * Most marketers determine the frequency of their email send based on past email engagement data. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2023) 
  * 87% of brands say that email marketing is very critical to business success. ( [ Litmus ](https://www.litmus.com/resources/state-of-email-workflows) , 2023) 
  * 77% of marketers have seen an increase in email engagement over the last 12 months. ( [ Not Another State of Marketing ](https://www.hubspot.com/state-of-marketing?_ga=2.120404555.2116605659.1655405116-782004359.1655405116) , 2021) 
  * Tuesdays are the best days to send an email for the highest performance, Sundays are the worst. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2023)   
  
![bar graph displaying the best days to send marketing emails](https://53.fs1.hubspotusercontent-na1.net/hub/53/hubfs/best%20day%20to%20send.jpg?width=1500&height=844&name=best%20day%20to%20send.jpg)
  * Most marketers say Apple’s Privacy Protection feature has not impacted the accuracy of the email marketing metrics they track. Those that have reported changes say it’s had the most impact on reporting accuracy for overall email traffic. (HubSpot Blog Research, 2023) 
  * Personalization using dynamic content is the top personalization tactic used by email marketing teams. It’s also the best way to improve email performance. ( [ Litmus ](https://www.litmus.com/resources/state-of-email-workflows) , 2023) 
  * The most effective email subject lines engage curiosity, include promotional offers, and are personalized to each recipient's interests. ( [ HubSpot Blog Research ](/marketing/hubspot-blog-marketing-industry-trends-report) , 2021) 
  * QA, A/B, and spam testing your emails leads to higher ROI. Enjoy up to a 28% higher return when you put testing to work for your email program. ( [ Litmus ](https://www.litmus.com/resources/email-marketing-roi/) , 2022) 

  * 85% of eCommerce marketers say their primary objective with email is increasing brand awareness. ( [ HubSpot Blog ](/marketing/email-marketing-guide) , 2023)

  * Emails with images offer an almost 10% boost in open rates.( [ GetResponse ](https://www.getresponse.com/resources/reports/email-marketing-benchmarks) , 2022)

###  B2B Email Marketing Statistics 

  * Email is the third-highest owned media platform B2B marketers used to distribute content in the past 12 months. ( [ Content Marketing Institute ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/articles/b2b-power-content-marketing-research/) , 2022) 
  * 81% of B2B marketers say their most used form of content marketing is email newsletters. ( [ Content Marketing Institute ](https://contentmarketinginstitute.com/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/2020_B2B_Research_Final.pdf) , 2020) 
  * 14.3% of all emails go missing or have been caught by popular spam filters. ( [ Email Tool Tester ](https://www.emailtooltester.com/en/email-deliverability-test/) , 2023) 
  * B2B marketers say that new product and feature announcement marketing emails have the highest click-through rate. ( [ HubSpot Blog Research ](/marketing/hubspot-blog-marketing-industry-trends-report) , 2021)
